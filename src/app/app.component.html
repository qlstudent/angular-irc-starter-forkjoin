Guidelines you must follow

Use this StackBlitz to reproduce your issue in a clear and simple way so we can help you the best we can.

Some people will refuse to help you if these are not respected, taking it as a lack of efforts on your side:

  • Make it work (while still demonstrating your issue). No one likes an example that doesn't work.
  • Make it as light as possible; Remove all unneeded code. No issue spans across many files of 400 lines of code each (true story). Your files having more than 400 lines is an issue in itself anyway.
  • Comment your code, detail where the issue is visible, the expected behavior vs. what is happening.
  • Don’t make assumptions or XY problems (e.g. “here is where it probably comes from”, “I want to do Y because of X”), just explain what actually does (not) happen when it should (not).
